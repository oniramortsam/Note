Convessità

  • Diseguaglianza Di Hölder-Riesz-Thorin:

    Dalla convessità di \(x^p\) per \(p \ge 1\) si ha: \begin{split} \frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k} &\le \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^p}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{p}\\ \sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k &\le \left(\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^p\right)^\frac{1}{p} \cdot \left(\sum_{k \in n} a_k\right)^\frac{p - 1}{p}\\ \sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k &\le \left(\sum_{k \in n} a_k^p\right)^\frac{1}{p} \cdot \left(\sum_{k \in n} b_k^\frac{p}{p - 1}\right)^\frac{p - 1}{p}. \end{split} Da cui otteniamo la diseguaglianza di Hölder.   • Diseguaglianze tra medie:

    Se \(0 < p \le q\), dalla convessità di \(x^\frac{q}{p}\) riscrivendo la prima si ottiene: \begin{split} \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^p}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{p} &\le \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^q}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{q}\\ \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^{- q}}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{- q} &\le \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^{- p}}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{- p}. \end{split} Dalla concavità di \(\log(x)\) si ha: \begin{split} \frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot \log (b_k)}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k} &\le \log \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)\\ \left(\prod_{k \in n} b_k^{a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k} &\le \frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\\ \left(\prod_{k \in n} b_k^{a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k} &\le \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^p}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{p}\\ \left(\frac{\sum_{k \in n} a_k \cdot b_k^{- p}}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{- p} &\le \left(\prod_{k \in n} b_k^{a_k}\right)^\frac{1}{\sum_{k \in n} a_k}. \end{split}

  • Diseguaglianza Di Young:

    Se \begin{split} \frac{1}{\alpha} + \frac{1}{\beta} + \frac{1}{\gamma} = 2, \end{split} allora (DIMOSTRAZIONE?) \begin{split} \sum_{k, h \in {\mathbb Z}} a_k b_h c_{k + h} \le \left(\sum_{k \in {\mathbb Z}} a_k^\alpha\right)^\frac{1}{\alpha} \cdot \left(\sum_{k \in {\mathbb Z}} b_k^\beta\right)^\frac{1}{\beta} \cdot \left(\sum_{k \in {\mathbb Z}} c_k^\gamma\right)^\frac{1}{\gamma} \end{split} Questa si generalizza al caso \begin{split} \sum_{i \in d} \frac{1}{\alpha_i} = d - 1, \end{split} come \begin{split} \sum_{k \in {\mathbb Z}^d \!: \sum_{i \in d} k_i = 0} \prod_{i \in d} a_{i, k_i} \le \prod_{i \in d} \left(\sum_{k \in {\mathbb Z}} a_{i, k_i}^{\alpha_i}\right)^\frac{1}{\alpha_i} \end{split}

  • Diseguaglianza di Gagliardo:

    Sia \(\pi_i: {\mathbb Z}^d \rightarrow {\mathbb Z}^{d - 1}\), la proiezione canonica, allora \begin{split} \sum_{{\rm k}\in{\mathbb Z}^{\rm d}}\prod_{{\rm i}\in{\rm d}}{\rm a}_{{\rm i},\pi_{\rm i}\cdot{\rm k}}\le\prod_{{\rm i}\in{\rm d}}\left(\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\mathbb Z}^{{\rm d}-1}}{\rm a}_{{\rm i},{\rm k}}^{{\rm d}-1}\right) \end{split}

  • Diseguaglianza di Hardy-Littlewood-Pólya:

    La diseguaglianza di riarrangiamento afferma che se \begin{split} a_k \le a_{k + 1}\\ b_k \le b_{k + 1}, \end{split} allora \begin{split} \sum_{k \le n} a_k b_{n - k} \le \sum_{k \le n} a_k b_{\sigma (k)} \le \sum_{k \le n} a_k b_k. \end{split}
